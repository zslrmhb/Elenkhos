ElenkhosServe

A from-scratch LLM inference engine and model (an owned Qwen3-0.6B forward + int4 quantization) — paged KV cache, Orca continuous batching, Sarathi-Serve chunked prefill, SLO-aware admission — benchmarked Mac/CPU/edge-first (Apple Silicon, CPU, and a Raspberry Pi 5) against llama.cpp, with an optional GPU leg vs vLLM/SGLang, then optimized with a paper-backed technique to outperform the production baseline in a defended regime. One focused inference-systems project over 12 weeks.
